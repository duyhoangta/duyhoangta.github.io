About me

I am a PIMS postdoc at the Compter Science Department, University of Victoria, hosted by Valerie King.
Previously, I recieved a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Oregon State University. I am fortunate enough to have Cora Borradaile as my advisor. I am interested in, but not limited to, graph theory and its applications to computer science. I have been working on problems in planar graphs and TSP in H-minor-free graphs.
Long time ago, I got a B.S. degree in Computer Science (honors program) from Hanoi University of Science and Technology.
